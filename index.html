About Us

Our History

Founded in 2017, the BC Youth Council was established by Fallon O'Neill with her best friend when she was 14 years old. She created the BC Youth Council to establish a network of youth aged 14-24 from across British Columbia that provides a platform for change across the province. This change is achieved through working closely with politicians and community organizations, sharing voices with other youth, and making differences both provincially and locally. In July 2020, we were incorporated and officially became a nonprofit.

BCYC Chapters

Student Reps Meetings

To discuss the importance of student trustees and the issue of student representation in British Columbia, the Student Reps Campaign at BCYC has met with government officials such as Patti Bacchus, Jordan Tinney, Nick Milum, Leah Bae, Senator Martin, Spencer Chandra-Herbet, Rob Fleming, Jennifer Reddy, and more!

Environmental Cleanups

The environmental team at BC Youth Council is dedicated to reducing single-use plastics in the community. In addition to creating petitions for by-laws, they have done this by organizing numerous park cleanups alongside a massive shoreline cleanup.

BC Youth Council Chats

In December 2021, our podcast—BC Youth Council Chats—was selected by Feedspot as one of the Top 20 Youth Empowerment Podcasts on the internet.
